General ledger (account) report
On the General Ledger (Account) Report page, you can generate consolidation general ledger reports that provide a comprehensive history of each account within the consolidation. These reports encompass the entire timeline of each account since the consolidation started, including its statutory value in the bundles and its contribution value in the consolidated accounts for each company within the group.

To access the General Ledger Account page, click Reports > Drill-Down Analysis > General Ledger (Account).

Create a general ledger account report:
1. In the Company and Account fields, enter the company and account codes. You can also click to find and select from their respective Select pop-up windows.
2. Optionally, in the Partner field, enter specific partner information you want to include or leave empty to include all partners.
3. Select a consolidation period. You can select either the Current or Reference consolidation period.
4. Click to display data relevant to your input in the table below. You will see all the individual lines that contribute to the total of the account in these column headings: Journal - Journal code, Entry - Adjustment number, Text - Journal description, Partner - Partner code, Curr - Currency in which the amount is expressed, Amount - Amount, Calc - When you select this for any individual line, the Total of the selected individual line(s), are calculated in the Selected Amount below the list.
5. You have these options: To view the details of each line, click on the line to display the data details where you can further do some drill-down analysis. You can export this data and save it for additional analysis.
6. In the Report options field, select a report type.
7. Click to generate the report. After the report is generated, a download link appears below for you to click and view it in the file type selected.

General ledger (account) – contributions
The General Ledger (Account) - Contributions page provides information about all the companies that contribute to the selected account displayed on the page. At the top of the Contributions page, you can find details about the selected account, such as its name or number. Additionally, you will see the total amount in the group currency for the current consolidation period and the reference consolidation period. The purpose of the Contributions page is to provide a consolidated view of how different companies participate in the selected account. It allows you to analyze the contributions made by various entities within the organization towards that particular account.

To access this page, on the page, click in the Navigation box.

This page displays the breakdown of the selected account per company. The list contains the following columns: The Company Code and Name, The Amount for the Current and the Reference Consolidation Periods, The Currency. Click to return to the page.

General ledger (account) – local amounts
The General Ledger (Account) – Local Amounts page shows all amounts that are available in the local currency for the selected company and account on the page. The selected company and account information is listed on top of the page. The total amounts in both local and group currencies for the current consolidation period and the reference consolidation period and the calculated ratio between the amounts in group currency and local currency are also displayed.

To access this page, on the page, click in the Navigation box.

This page displays a breakdown of the selected account per company. The list contains the following columns: The journal type, entry (category), and description; The Amount for the Current and the Reference Consolidation Period. Click to return to the page.

Drill-Down Analysis
The Drill-Down Analysis allows for detailed data analysis and viewing contributions by company or local amounts. Access the Drill-Down Analysis page to further analyze and export data for additional insights.

To access the Drill-Down Analysis page, click Reports > Drill-Down Analysis.

To sort the data, go to the Sort on box and make a selection: The and buttons allow you to go back and forth the list by Accounts or Companies.

To view contributions by Company or Local amounts, go to the Navigation box and make a selection: opens the page, displaying all the companies contributing to the selected account; opens the page, displaying all the amounts for the selected account that are available in the local currency of the company.
